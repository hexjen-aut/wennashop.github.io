The Story

Extracted from the kernels of the argan tree (Argania spinosa), endemic to the semi-desert Souss valley of Morocco. This "liquid gold" is hand-pressed by local cooperatives using techniques passed down through generations. Rich in vitamin E and fatty acids, it serves as an exceptional ritual for skin rejuvenation and hair vitality.
